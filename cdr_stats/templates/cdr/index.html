{% blocktrans %} Info {% endblocktrans %}


CDR-Stats {% blocktrans %}is an open source application that allows you to browse and analyze your CDRs (Call Details Record). {% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %}Different reporting tools are provided :{% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans %}Monthly Report : summarize your monthly traffic and allow you to have a monthly vision of your traffic load.{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}Analyze CDR : This report allows you to compare your traffic with previous day and study the volum variation on your server{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}Daily Traffic : give you a day by day vision and let you see how many calls are running each hours{% endblocktrans %}
