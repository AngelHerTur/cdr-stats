Info


CDR-Stats is an open source application that allows you to browse and analyze your CDRs (Call Details Record).
Different reporting tools are provided :

  • Monthly Report :
    summarize your monthly traffic and allow you to have a monthly vision of your traffic load.
  • Analyze CDR : This report allows you to compare your traffic with previous day and study the volum variation on your server
  • Daily Traffic : give you a day by day vision and let you see how many calls are running each hours



Download

Please visit http://www.cdr-stats.org/
and download the last stable release.
